## What CleanApp Is

CleanApp is building a global sensor and routing layer for **problem signals** (physical and digital). The pipeline:
1. **Intake**: Humans and agents spot problems and opportunities — bugs, incidents, scams, UX friction, safety hazards, improvement proposals
2. **Analysis**: AI filters, deduplicates, clusters, and extracts structure from raw signals
3. **Distribution**: Processed intelligence is routed to whoever can act — owners, maintainers, operators, stakeholders
